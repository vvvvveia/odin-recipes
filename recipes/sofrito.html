Sofrito

Some of the ingredients that would work well in sofrito.

Ingredients

  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 bunch of fresh cilantro
  • 6 cloves of garlic

It is worth nothing that while these are fairly common ingredients that you can use to make sofrito, you can add or include whatever ingredients are calling you. Get creative with it!

Directions

  1. Peel the onion and deseed bell pepper, then quarter them. Rise the cilantro and chop the bunch roughly.
  2. Add the onion, bell pepper, cilantro, and garlic to the bowl of a food processor. Pulse until a smooth, thick puree forms.
  3. Use the sofrito in a recipe immediately, store in an air-tight container for up to a month in the fridge, or portion into ice cube trays and store in the freezer for up to 3 months.
